// Platform Modules

Built for the complexity of professional field operations.

Liability Management
Incident logs, compliance documentation, and waiver integration — structured for audit. Connect your existing waiver provider or link directly. Every event on record.
Guide & Staff Logistics
Certifications, availability, trip assignments, and credential expiry — unified in a single roster view. Built for multi-guide operations at scale.
Field Operations Blueprint
Route permitting, weather windows, gear manifests, and evacuation protocols — structured as operational infrastructure, not notes in a group chat.
Operations Management
Program administrators build and deploy field operations — assigning guides, instructors, and support staff with defined roles, responsibilities, and trip-specific protocols.
Operational Reporting
Incident trends, guide utilization, revenue per route, and compliance status — surfaced as institutional-grade reporting, not a basic dashboard.
Compliance Infrastructure
Built-in frameworks for land agency permit compliance, insurer documentation, and multi-jurisdiction regulatory requirements. Defensible by design.
