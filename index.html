// Platform Modules

Built for the compliance demands of guiding operations and youth programs.

Liability Management
Incident logs, compliance documentation, and waiver integration — structured for audit. Every event on record, time-stamped, and exportable in 30 seconds for your insurance carrier or parent inquiry.
Guide & Staff Logistics
Guide certifications, WFR/WAFA expiry, trip assignments, and availability — unified in a single roster view. No more chasing credentials in a spreadsheet the morning of departure.
Field Operations Blueprint
Route permitting, weather windows, gear manifests, and evacuation protocols — structured as operational infrastructure, not notes in a group chat.
Operations Management
Program administrators build and deploy field operations — assigning guides, instructors, and support staff with defined roles, responsibilities, and trip-specific protocols.
Operational Reporting
Incident trends, guide utilization, revenue per route, and compliance status — surfaced as institutional-grade reporting, not a basic dashboard.
Compliance Infrastructure
Built-in frameworks for USFS and BLM permit compliance, insurer documentation, and AMGA accreditation support. If your operation gets audited tomorrow, AlpenOS is ready.

AlpenOS is built to your operation — your permit structure, your EAPs, your staff roles, and your route library. We do the setup. You show up to training.
