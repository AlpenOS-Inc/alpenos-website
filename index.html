// Four Stages. Every Operation.

Built around how field operations actually work.

From the planning session weeks out to the debrief in the parking lot. Here's what that looks like inside the platform.

01 — Design
Build the op before it goes live.
+
Map the route. Assign roles. Attach your EAP. Set the gear manifest. Build the pre-departure checklist with task-level scripts so every guide knows not just what to do — but how to do it. Program administrators design once. The platform runs it every time.
Route & location library Gear manifests EAP templates Trigger checklist builder Task assignment with scripts Pre-departure protocols
02 — Deploy
Put the right people on the right trips. Verified.
+
Assign guides with credential matching — WFR, WFA, WEMT, AMGA certs checked automatically. Confirm participant rosters, outstanding waivers, and medical forms before the van leaves the parking lot. Departure readiness isn't a gut check. It's a dashboard.
Guide credential tracking Cert expiry alerts Participant roster Waiver completion status Pre-departure readiness dashboard Role-based staff access
03 — Log
What happens in the field stays in the record.
+
One tap to log a near-miss. Incident observations captured as they happen — not reconstructed hours later from memory. Weather call documented with rationale. Itinerary deviation logged with approval. Field notes attach to the route for future operations.

Alpen/OS works offline — everything captured in the field syncs the moment your team returns to service. The record doesn't depend on cell signal. Neither does your operation.
One-tap field incident log Near-miss reporting Weather decision workflow Itinerary deviation log Offline capture + auto-sync Field debrief prompt
04 — Close
Every trip closes with a complete record.
+
The debrief prompt fires before guides leave the parking lot. Gear returns are logged against the manifest. Incident reports generate from field inputs — no re-entry required. Post-trip notes attach to the route for next season.

Six months from now, when someone asks what happened on that trip, the answer is a search, not a scramble.
Async post-trip debrief Gear return + condition log Auto-generated incident reports Route condition notes Seasonal institutional memory Exportable compliance record
